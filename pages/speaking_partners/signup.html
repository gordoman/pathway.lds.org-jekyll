Speaking Partners

Sign Up / Find a Partner


Ready to improve the life of a Pathway student by volunteering for just 30 minutes each week as a speaking partner mentor?

Here's How to Get Started:

Requirements

  • Speak English fluently
  • Be a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, a BYU-Idaho student, or a Pathway student
  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Participate in a 30-minute appointment once a week for 12 consecutive weeks.

Getting Started

Speaking partner meetings begin the second week of every semester and last through the last week of the semester:
Fall mid September-late December
Winter mid January-mid April
Spring late April-late July
