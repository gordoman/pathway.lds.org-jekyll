L Version

For students who have intermediate-low English proficiency

How Pathway Works

Online Academic Courses

During each of the three semesters, students take just one online academic class from BYU-Idaho. The three academic classes incorporate English-language instruction as a main component of the coursework and are designed to give students the confidence and skills needed to succeed in college and life. The L version academic courses include Pathway English and Life Skills (GS 120L), English Language and Personal Finance (Math 100L), and English Language and Basic Writing (ENG 106L).

Religion Courses

Because of the advantages for English-learning Church members, L version students are strongly encouraged to enroll in an institute class of their choice during the three semesters of Pathway.

Weekly Gathering

The program includes a 90-minute gathering each week at a local institute facility or church meetinghouse with other Pathway students and a service missionary couple.

The gathering provides social, emotional, and academic support and is held every Thursday evening during the three BYU-Idaho academic semesters.
