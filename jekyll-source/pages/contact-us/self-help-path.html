PATH

What is PATH?

PATH is the name of Pathway’s administrative system. In PATH, missionaries and Pathway employees can access information about individual Pathway sites, students, and more.

How can I see new applicants to my Pathway site?

Missionaries can track new applicants under the “Site Manager” link in Pathway’s administrative system. Click here for a tutorial.

Missionaries can view student information under the “Site Manager” link in Pathway’s administrative system. Click here for a tutorial.
