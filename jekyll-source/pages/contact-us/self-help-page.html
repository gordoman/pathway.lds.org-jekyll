General Pathway

What is Pathway?

Pathway is a low-cost educational opportunity that mixes the flexibility of online courses with the benefits of local student-to-student interaction. Students earn college credit that is eligible for transfer to BYU-Idaho and other universities. The program takes one year (or three semesters) to complete and can lead to professional certificates and degrees. Admission is open to members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in areas where Pathway is established. For more information, visit: pathway.lds.org/main/information.

Can I get an identification card from BYU-Idaho?

No, BYU-Idaho identification cards are not available for Pathway students.

What is Pathway’s privacy policy?

See Section 6.5 of the Pathway Handbook.

How/where can I find my student ID number?

Students can find their Pathway student ID number by signing in at pathway.lds.org. Once logged in, students should click the arrow next to their name (top right corner). Their student ID number will appear at the top of the dropdown list.

Requesting/Implementing a new Pathway Site

How can I get Pathway in my area?

The Pathway Home Office determines new Pathway locations in coordination with local LDS priesthood leaders. Priesthood leaders who are interested in bringing Pathway to their area should contact Pathway Support. Other individuals who want to bring Pathway to their area should submit a request through their local priesthood leaders.

What is my role as a Pathway missionary?

Pathway missionaries play a vital part in fulfilling the purposes and objectives of Pathway. Missionaries’ primary responsibilities include shepherding enrolled students, informing Church members about Pathway’s unique opportunities and blessings, and facilitating weekly gatherings with Pathway students.

Other Topics

Student Honor

Are Pathway students required to live the BYU-Idaho Honor Code?

Pathway students are encouraged to strive to live the BYU-Idaho Honor Code, but are not required to do so as a condition of enrollment. (However, Pathway students who join the BYU-Idaho Online Degree Program after Pathway will be required to live the BYU-Idaho Honor Code.)

Tutoring

Are tutors available if I have trouble with my homework?

Yes. BYU-Idaho provides free tutoring to Pathway students for most academic subjects. Information about how to sign up for a tutor is found within eligible Pathway courses under the “Course” folder, then “Resources.”
