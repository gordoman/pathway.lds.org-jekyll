Disabilities

Are disability accommodations available to Pathway students?

Disability accommodations may be available to students who qualify. For more information, visit www.byui.edu/disabilities/pathway-and-disabilities.



PATH

What is PATH?

PATH is the name of Pathway’s administrative system. In PATH, missionaries and Pathway employees can access information about individual Pathway sites, students, and more.

How can I see new applicants to my Pathway site?

Missionaries can track new applicants under the “Site Manager” link in Pathway’s administrative system. Click here for a tutorial.

How can I find a student’s phone number and email address?

Missionaries can view student information under the “Site Manager” link in Pathway’s administrative system. Click here for a tutorial.

How do I set up groups?

How do I access “New Missionary Orientation” or other training resources?

Student Honor

Are Pathway students required to live the BYU-Idaho Honor Code?

Pathway students are encouraged to strive to live the BYU-Idaho Honor Code, but are not required to do so as a condition of enrollment. (However, Pathway students who join the BYU-Idaho Online Degree Program after Pathway will be required to live the BYU-Idaho Honor Code.)
