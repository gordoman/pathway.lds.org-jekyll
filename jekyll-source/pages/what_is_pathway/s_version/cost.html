  • Argentina
  • Chile
If you have any questions regarding possible taxation resulting from your participation in Pathway, we suggest that you contact a tax advisor.

Pathway students who pursue a degree through BYU-Idaho's Online Degree Program after Pathway continue to pay their local Pathway tuition rate. Pathway tuition may increase annually.

Pathway students who choose to continue their education on campus after Pathway will pay regular on-campus tuition.

Financial Aid

Financial aid is not available for Pathway students. However, U.S. students who have a high school diploma or GED may apply for federal financial aid after they complete Pathway and if they are accepted into a degree-seeking program.

Local Taxes

As a result of your Pathway participation, taxes or other payments may be owed to your local government. It is your responsibility to understand those laws and comply. Click here to see which countries may require tax payments.
