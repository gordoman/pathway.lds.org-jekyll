For students who have intermediate-low English proficiency

Speaking Partners

Using online video chat technology such as Skype, students meet weekly with a native English speaker throughout the three semesters to improve their English skills. By spending 30 minutes each week in English conversation with a fluent speaker, along with 30 minutes of practice with a peer, students significantly increase their English skills over the course of the program.
